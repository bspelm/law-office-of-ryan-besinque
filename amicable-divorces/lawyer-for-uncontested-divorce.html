Easy Divorce in NY

The quickest way to get a divorce in New York is by filing for an uncontested case and having both parties agree on all issues, highlighting the importance of hiring a lawyer for uncontested divorce. This type of divorce is much faster and cheaper than contested divorces, which can take months to a year to reach a resolution. However, even uncontested cases have a number of outside factors that can affect the timeline.

The first step in any divorce in New York is filing the necessary paperwork with the county clerk’s office. This can be done by hand or over the internet using NYSCEF, which is the state’s e-filing system. The person who files for the divorce is called the Plaintiff and the spouse they are divorcing is known as the Defendant. It is here where the skills of a lawyer for uncontested divorce can be crucial.

Once the paperwork has been filed, it can then be served on the Defendant. Involving a lawyer for uncontested divorce ensures that this step is done correctly. In most cases this will be done by a private process server, but in some counties it can be done electronically. It is important that the Defendant receives the papers within 40 days or they can default by failing to respond. A lawyer can be helpful in ensuring that the necessary paperwork is served correctly and in a timely manner.

After the Defendant has been served, they have 40 days to file an answer to the lawsuit or else they can automatically default on all of the divorce’s issues. Consulting a lawyer for uncontested divorce can simplify this process for both parties. The main issue will be the division of assets, and if there are children involved, the custody issues as well. The court will need to determine an equitable settlement for both parties and the amount of spousal support, child support or visitation payments that are needed.

The process is much longer when there is a contested divorce because the court will need to review the facts and arguments of both sides before making a decision. There may also be a need to hold a trial and/or hearings before a judge will sign off on the case. Even with an uncontested divorce, a lawyer for uncontested divorce can ensure that all agreements are fair and legally binding.

Even with an uncontested divorce, it can take a few months to a year to finalize all of the issues. The involvement of a lawyer for uncontested divorce can expedite this process. The amount of time is based on how cooperative both parties are with each other, and how willing they are to compromise during the negotiations. The other factor that can impact the timeline is how backed up the courts are, which can delay even uncontested divorce cases. It is a good idea to consult with a qualified New York divorce attorney to ensure that all of the proper steps are taken in a timely fashion. Having a knowledgeable attorney to help with the process can significantly reduce the chances of errors, which can delay the case. It can also save time and money for the client in the long run.
